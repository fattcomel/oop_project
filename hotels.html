Top 5 Hotels to stay in France

Hotel Lutetia

Hotel Lutetia is an iconic luxury hotel situated in the Saint-Germain-des-Prés area. Located on the Left Bank of the River Seine, it is a 5-minute walk from le Bon Marché Department Store and 2 km from the Louvre Museum.




Le Damantin Hôtel & Spa

Set in the 8th arr. District of Paris, Le Damantin Hôtel & Spa offers free access to the spa & wellness centre, which includes an indoor pool and a sauna. Built in 19th century, the property is within 1.5 km of Orangerie Museum. The accommodation features a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi is available.




InterContinental Paris Le Grand, an IHG Hotel

Intercontinental Paris Le Grand is located just 30 m from the Opéra Garnier and Opera Metro Station. It offers 2 restaurants and a health club with sauna and massage treatments.




Manolita Paris

Situated 500 m from Pigalle Metro Station, Manolita Paris offers 5-star accommodation in Paris and has a bar. This 5-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The property is allergy-free and is located 600 m from La Cigale Concert Hall.




Paris j'Adore Hotel & Spa

Set in Paris, Paris j'Adore Hotel & Spa offers 5-star accommodation with a restaurant and a bar. Featuring a terrace, the 5-star hotel has air-conditioned rooms with free WiFi, each with a private bathroom. The accommodation offers room service, a concierge service and luggage storage for guests.
